Robotics & Hardware

We look forward to introduce advanced robotics workshops to students and engage them in different funded projects. Their main target will be to develop feasible projects from which they can produce some quality research papers later. The cost should be minimized and should target user convenience first. Students will collaborate & gain a better sense of design thinking.


Development of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) drones and autonomous robots will be prioritized according to the new trend.

Software & Game development

Students are expected to develop some useful mobile applications and web applications. They will figure out new ways of applying different software design patterns to target and solve various problems.


2D and 3D game development are the source of entertainment nowadays. Some games can also act like a real time simulation of a certain space or place. Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented reality(AR) will be more emphasized. Students are expected to come up with some Mixed Reality (MR) games too.

Humanitarian

The projects will be developed for humanitarian purposes. Any kind of research or prototype (both hardware and software) that can solve any problem related to human life, global warming, earth issues will be encouraged.

Our History

The DU Innovation and Incubation Lab ( SecureU ), an initiative of the VC office of the University of Dhaka started its journey on Friday, May 06, 2016 to address various social challenges, and create and develop businesses through innovation in Bangladesh.



The DU Innovation and Incubation Lab (SecureU) will be a facility where students and faculty members from the different disciplines (e.g. Arts, Business, Science, and Social Science etc.) and employees of the University of Dhaka can collaborate and work together to address various social challenges, and create and develop businesses through innovative products/services/solutions in Bangladesh.



Our Mission

1. Idea Generation and Implementation (IGE) Generate innovative business and social ideas to address social and economic needs; guide and facilitate entrepreneurs towards establishing enterprises; facilitation of networking between emerging entrepreneurs and the business community



2. Training and Supports (TS): provide customized training/workshops on innovation, offer mentoring and advising support on different challenges and opportunities to successfully launch and manage the enterprises.



3. Collaboration and Engagement (CE): establish linkages with various national and international centers/organizations e.g. centers and labs of different universities, private sector companies working on innovation and other stakeholders, and play an active role in sharing the lessons from both global and local best practices towards successful implementation in Bangladesh.
